Quotation


1. blockquote element defines the section which is quoted from another sites.

For nearly 60 years, WWF has been protecting the future of nature. The world's leading conservation organization, WWF works in 100 countries and is supported by more than one million members in the United States and close to five million globally.

2. q tag provides quotation mark.

For nearly 60 years, WWF has been protecting the future of nature. The world's leading conservation organization, WWF works in 100 countries and is supported by more than one million members in the United States and close to five million globally.


3. cite is usually used to define title of creative work.

The Scream by Edvard Munch. Painted in 1893.


4. abbr tag gives useful informatio to the browser.

The WHO was founded in 1948.


5. The HTML address tag defines the contact information for the author/owner of a document or an article. The contact information can be an email address, URL, physical address, phone number, social media handle, etc. element usually renders in italic

Written by John Doe.
